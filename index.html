AI Camp 2017 Winner

AI Camp 2017 - Winner

This summer Kainos, held its first Artificial Intelligence camp. This camp compromised of two weeks of training concluding with a hackathon.

I lead a team of three to develop an idea of mine for a software solution to optimise recruitment in the IT sector, Catalyst. Catalyst uses feedback from those currently employed in the industry to match users to a role which may best suit them.

Catalyst uses machine learning techniques such as one hot encoding, SGDClassifier and other sklearn functionality in addition to a user friendly front end in the form of a chatbot. I was responsible for the backend of the system, the branding and I also lead the pitch.

Our team was placed first overall by a team of industry experts and were commended on the product we had produced.

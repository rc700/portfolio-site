AI Camp 2017 Winner

AI Camp 2017 - 1st Place

This summer Kainos, held its first Artificial Intelligence camp. This camp compromised of two weeks of training concluding with a hackathon.

I lead a team of three to develop an idea of mine for a software solution to optimise recruitment in the IT sector, Catalyst. Catalyst uses feedback from those currently employed in the industry to match users to a role which may best suit them.

Catalyst uses machine learning techniques such as one hot encoding, SGDClassifier and other sklearn functionality in addition to a user friendly front end in the form of a chatbot. I was responsible for the backend of the system, the branding and I also lead the pitch.

Our team was placed first overall by a team of industry experts and were commended on the product we had produced.

Inside Business Radio Interview

Inside Business Radio Interview

I was selected to feature on a panel to discuss the future of Artificial Intelligence and how it will change both jobs and our everyday lives. Other members of the panel included: Tom Gray, CTO of Kainos; Austin Tanney, Head of Life Science at Analytics Engines and Dr Michaela Black, Head of Computing and Intelligent Systems at Ulster University.

Placement Student at SAP Belfast

Software Development Intern at SAP

I underwent a year in industry with SAP, the world's leading provider of e-business software solutions and the world's third largest software company. I was based in their Belfast Office and worked directly with senior developers, learning from their experience and developing my skills. Through this internship I was responsible for software design, prototyping, developing and testing and gained valuable experience with Python, Django, OpenStack, GIT, Netapp and Linux.
I worked mainly on the integration of both Netapp and MapR into the Cloud Frame Manager on which HANA runs.
